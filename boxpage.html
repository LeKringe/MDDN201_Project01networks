EXTERNAL DEVICES

To power a city such as Wellington, an effective electric power distribution system is needed, forming a complex network of components and parts around the city. Below are a few that are easily recognizable and can be found all over Wellington.

POWER BOX

This electric power box is one of many in a network of other power boxes that help to distribute electricity around the city. It is essentially an electrical transformer that is used in conjunction with the underground electrical distribution.

CABLES

These are protective plastic cases that shield fragile electric cables from the elements. The cables act as a connector between power boxes and the rest of the network of the local power distribution system.

WALL MOUNTED POWER BOX

Essentially it is a smaller version of the power box, and serves the same functions. However, this is used for smaller shops and stores, as the power usage is not as great as a building, which warrants a smaller power box to do the job. This power box in particular seems to have had many stickers added to it's surface.
